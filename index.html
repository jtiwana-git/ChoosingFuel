Choosing fuels types

I decided to create a website that will help me and others to choose the right fuel type for their vehicle. Back in the days, there were only two fuel types to choose from which were petrol and diesel. But now, there are more fuel types to choose from. This website will provide information about each fuel type and also provide the pros and cons of each fuel type. This will help you to choose the right fuel type for your vehicle. Users can share their thoughts and experiences in the comment section on each page.
